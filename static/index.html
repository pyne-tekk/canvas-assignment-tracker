Setup guide
Canvas
Finding your domain
Log into Canvas in your browser. The domain is the hostname in the URL bar — everything before the first slash after https://. It usually looks like school.instructure.com.
Getting an API token
  1. Click your profile picture in the top-right corner of Canvas.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Scroll down to Approved Integrations.
  4. Click New Access Token, give it any purpose, no expiry.
  5. Copy the token and paste it into Slate. You will not be able to see it again after closing the dialog.
Infinite Campus
NCEDCloud credentials
Your username is your student ID number. Your password is your NCEDCloud password — the same one you use to log into school computers or ncedcloud.mcnc.org. This is not your Canvas login.
Finding your IC domain
Log into Infinite Campus directly through your school's portal. The domain is the hostname from the URL, for example:
600.ncsis.gov district.infinitecampus.org
Email Alerts
Sent to your account email after each sync. All off by default.
